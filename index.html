Who is MarieAsh?

Ashley Hall is a master stylist whose passion is to help promote hair growth and insure your hair is healthy. She was born in raised in Cleveland Ohio and has always enjoyed doing hair as a child. She relocated in 2013 to Charlotte NC to pursue her career as a stylist and hope to make an amazing difference in the hair community to not only help women embrace their hair, but to guide them to love their natural hair.
